Hygiene Tips for Period & PCOD

1. Maintain Personal Hygiene

Change pads or tampons every 4-6 hours to prevent infections.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drinking water helps regulate your cycle and reduces bloating.

3. Eat a Balanced Diet

Include iron-rich foods and avoid junk food to maintain hormonal balance.

4. Use Mild Intimate Wash

Avoid harsh soaps; use pH-balanced washes to prevent infections.

5. Wear Breathable Cotton Underwear

Wearing tight or synthetic fabric can trap moisture, causing irritation.

6. Proper Disposal of Sanitary Products

Wrap pads/tampons properly before disposing to maintain hygiene.

7. Avoid Using Scented Products

Deodorants or scented wipes may disrupt the natural pH balance.

8. Do Light Exercises & Yoga

Regular movement helps with PCOD symptoms and reduces cramps.

9. Track Your Periods

Tracking helps you understand cycle changes and PCOD symptoms.

10. Visit a Gynecologist Regularly

Regular check-ups help in early detection of any reproductive health issues.
